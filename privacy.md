### **Zapplebot Privacy Policy**

Zapplebot is a privately-operated Discord bot. By using Zapplebot, interacting with it, or inviting it to a server, you agree to the following policy regarding data collection and use.

---

#### **1. Data Collected**

Zapplebot may process or store the following types of data:

* Messages sent directly to the bot (commands, inputs, prompts)
* Server metadata (server name, user count, channel names)
* User identifiers such as Discord usernames or user IDs
* Interaction logs (timestamps, usage frequency, command history)

Zapplebot **does not intentionally store** message history outside of interactions with the bot, but may temporarily or permanently log data for debugging, functionality, or improvement.

---

#### **2. Purpose of Data Use**

By using Zapplebot, you give permission for data processed by the bot to be:

* Logged for debugging, maintenance, analysis, or development
* Used for performance improvements, new features, or behavior tuning
* Reviewed manually by the bot operator (Zac Skalko)

This includes the ability to **retain, review, and reuse** data for development and improvement purposes.

---

#### **3. Data Sharing**

Data collected by Zapplebot is **not sold** or shared with third-party organizations.
However, **the bot operator may disclose data** if required for:

* Security or abuse investigation
* Legal compliance (if ever applicable)
* Debugging or performance analysis

---

#### **4. Data Retention**

Data may be retained for an **indefinite period** unless explicitly requested to be deleted.

Users may request removal of logged data **by contacting:**
GitHub: [https://github.com/zapplebee](https://github.com/zapplebee)
Twitter/X: [https://twitter.com/zapplebee](https://twitter.com/zapplebee)

Requests will be handled on a reasonable-effort basis.

---

#### **5. No Warranty**

Zapplebot is provided *“as is”* and **no guarantees** are made regarding privacy, security, reliability, or uptime.
